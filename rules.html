Meteeor's Rules and Guidelines

1• Follow the Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. The ToS can be found at the bottom of the rules. Conducting or discussing server raiding on or off of this server will result in a permanent ban for everyone involved.

2• Be Respectful. Respect all users. Do not harass other users in the server or in DMs. Do not intentionally troll, don't be annoying.

3• Do not spam. Do not send random or irrelevant messages. Excessive spam such as copypasta will result in an instant ban. This also applies to voice channels, being an annoyance will result in a punishment.

4• Do not advertise or self-promote . Do not ask other users for donations or money. You will receive a permanent ban if we determine you are using the server for those purposes.

5• No posting unsafe links. Do not post sketchy links, or links to malicious websites, pirated software, cracks etc. Do not upload non-image/video files. Do not post shortened links.

6• Do not post Not Safe For Work (NSFW) content . Do not link, or post images that are NSFW. Do not post text that is overtly NSFW.

7• No inappropriate profiles. Users with bad names will be nicknamed at the discretion of the Staff member. Bad names include zalgo, blank names, unusual unicode, political views, attention seeking names, racial slurs, or any other offensive material. NSFW avatars will result in an instant ban. Advertising in playing status will result in punishment upon discovery.

8• Speak English. This is an English speaking server, so speak English, please.

9• Respect channel topics. Every text channel has a specific purpose, so stay on topic.

10• No micro-modding. It's fine to provide accurate information to help members, but leave the moderating to the Staff Members.

NOTE: Rule 4 does not apply in Meteeor Advertising!!!
NOTE: Staff Members have the final say on rules. If a Staff member asks you to stop, you should stop.
NOTE: All rules apply to direct messages as well. If a user is harassing you and is in this server, then please contact staff with proof.
